Frequently Asked Questions

How do you match me with a VA?

▼
After your intake call, we review our roster of trained VAs to find the one whose skills, tools, and industry experience best match your needs. We typically have a match ready for you within 24–48 hours.

What tasks can my VA handle?

▼
Our VAs are trained in email management, scheduling, CRM data entry, lead follow-up, research, document preparation, and more. If it's a digital task that can be delegated, they can likely handle it.

Is there a long-term contract?

▼
No. All our plans are month-to-month. We believe in earning your business every single month through high-quality support and results.

What happens if my VA is sick or away?

▼
We have a backup system in place. If your VA is unavailable, their supervisor steps in to ensure critical tasks are still handled, or we provide a temporary replacement so your business doesn't skip a beat.
